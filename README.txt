*** This repo runs a univariate analysis and multivariate analysis on 
nuero medical imaging features and report features to predict adverse effects
specifically ARIA-E and ARIA-H. ***

Files and their function:
feature_extraction.py - is a radiology report parser that extracts structured clinical data from free-text MRI reports and outputs it as CSV. It's designed to convert unstructured radiological narratives into organized, queryable data for research purposes.
